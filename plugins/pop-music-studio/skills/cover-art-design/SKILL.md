# Cover Art Design

## Overview

Use this skill for structured cover-art planning outputs.

Read these files in order:

1. `../../references/shared-pipeline-rules.md`
2. `../../templates/cover-art-template.md`

## Required Workflow

1. Restate the release brief, sonic identity, and emotional target.
2. Define the core visual concept before layout or prompt details.
3. Specify composition, subject styling, palette, lighting, and typography.
4. Produce prompt seeds or asset requirements that match the concept.
5. Preserve MV handoff notes, risks, and open questions.

## Required Content

The final output must cover, when relevant:

- stage brief
- visual concept summary
- art references and mood words
- composition and subject direction
- palette, lighting, and texture notes
- typography strategy
- prompt seeds or asset checklist
- delivery notes for single, EP, or promo use
- risks and unresolved questions

## Quality Bar

- do not output image prompts with no concept explanation
- do not ignore the song's emotional and lyrical motifs
- do not use vague color words without composition guidance
- do not hide missing visual decisions; mark them as `To be confirmed`

## Common Failure Modes

Avoid these outputs:

- a generic aesthetic list with no cover composition
- visuals that do not match the artist persona or sonic era
- typography that fights the cover focal point
- artwork directions that cannot hand off cleanly to MV development
