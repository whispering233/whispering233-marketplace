# MV Generation

## Overview

Use this skill for structured MV-planning outputs.

Read these files in order:

1. `../../references/shared-pipeline-rules.md`
2. `../../templates/mv-template.md`

## Required Workflow

1. Restate the release brief, song arc, artist persona, and visual direction.
2. Define the MV concept before scene and shot details.
3. Break the video into scenes or beat groups with clear purpose.
4. Specify performance direction, camera ideas, styling, and edit rhythm.
5. Preserve delivery specs, risks, and open questions.

## Required Content

The final output must cover, when relevant:

- stage brief
- MV concept summary
- narrative or performance framework
- scene-by-scene plan
- shot ideas and camera language
- styling and location direction
- edit rhythm and transition notes
- delivery specs and production checklist
- risks and unresolved questions

## Quality Bar

- do not output only a vague storyline
- do not skip scene-level intent or shot guidance
- do not detach the MV from the song's hook, pacing, or visual identity
- do not hide missing production details; mark them as `To be confirmed`

## Common Failure Modes

Avoid these outputs:

- a moodboard with no usable scene structure
- a shot list with no narrative or performance logic
- visual ideas that contradict the cover art and artist persona
- edit notes that do not respect the music's dynamics and section changes
